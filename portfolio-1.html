Birthday Gift Card Design

For the One4all tenth birthday celebration, we were given a brief to make a special edition Birthday Gift Card. We had to come up with the design that will clearly show it is for Birthday. If it would be sitting on their Gift Card Mall amongst all the other gift card designs, it should evoke an impulse purchase. We also had to set the target market for each design we make. I wanted to catch an eye from pretty wide range of people, that is why I made 4 different designs.
